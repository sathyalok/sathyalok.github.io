Senior Citizen Cottages with Pathways

Home for Senior Citizens

Under the scheme of expansion programmes, Sathyalok started building a Home for senior citizens in the year 1987. The purpose of this venture was to care for the well being of senior citizens, who were alone for various reasons, such as their children migrating to other countries for better jobs etc, and were left without physical or economic support. As of today, we are have ninety senior citizens happily living with us.

In this project, we provide the following types of accommodation to senior citizens at nominal charges for boarding, lodging and other infrastructure. It enables them to lead a stress-free life. The nutritious food and comforts provided will help increase the longevity of their lives.

Types of accommodation provided at our Home for senior citizens are:

  • Dormitory
  • Double occupancy
  • Single occupancy
  • Deluxe double occupancy rooms
  • Cottages - fully furnished independent accommodation

Hygienic and nutritious vegetarian food is served in dining halls, which are provided with star hotel level furniture, sofa sets, televisions etc. Food is served at regular intervals, keeping their health in mind.

The senior citizens are provided with indoor games, such as Table Tennis, cards etc,.  Meditation and Yoga classes are also conducted on a regular basis.

Our Open-air Auditorium with ramp and Conference Hall adds much more to periodical entertainment and educative programmes.

Our Medicare centre provides 24 hours service with 12 beds and with Senior Doctors and trained nursing staff. The Home for senior citizens is surrounded by international temples, Bhajan Hall, Reading Room, well laid walking tracks as well as a Recreation centre.
