Vocational Training Program

Sathyalok realises that the educational institutions under the management of the Trust should give importance to "Educare". This refers to bringing out and nurturing the inherent talents of a child. Each child is born with certain talents, which the teachers recognise and motivate so that the child can flourish in life by using these inherent talents.

In order to emphasise and develop the children's talents, Sathyalok offers vocational training programs in addition to academic courses. Under this program, the Trust is training the children in fields such as:

  • Carpentry
  • Moulding / Welding
  • Painting
  • Refrigeration
  • Interior decoration
  • Electronic
  • Tailoring / Packing
  • Computer basics
