Our classroom with specialized equipment for hearing-challenged children


 

In 1984, our school for special needs children was established to serve as a rehabilitation centre for  mentally-challenged and multi-challenged children.

We started with three children. Today, we've grown to 200.

Our special needs school operates for both boys and girls, between the ages of 5 to 15 years old. Our facility is recognized by the Government of the State of Tamilnadu.

Since many of our children are from economically weaker sections of society, some of them are also sponsored by our charitable trust.

 

 

The school children have access to our music and cultural centre, computer coaching lessons, fine arts & crafts, carpentry and interior decoration facilities, etc.

Our school children have participated in state and inter-district competitions and have won many prizes and rolling shields. 

We believe in all-round development being important to let these children flourish in a modern, competitive society.
