Hostel for Students, Working Women and Nursing Students

In the year 1999, with the financial assistance of the Government of Japan, Sathyalok expanded its wings to take care of college going girls and working women by providing Hostel facilities, including boarding & lodging with well furnished single and multi occupancy rooms under the supervision of a well experienced warden. All girls are from low-income families and a nominal amount is charged.

  • The hostel is furbished with study lawns, a reading room, recreation hall, and medical-aid
  • The students are trained with good discipline and orderliness in all respects. More than 150 working women and students are under this majestic project.
